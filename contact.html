FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our programs and services.

How quickly will I hear back after submitting the contact form?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, we recommend calling our office directly at 071 123 0001.

Do you offer customized training programs for organizations?

Yes! We specialize in creating tailored communication training programs for businesses, educational institutions, and organizations. Our corporate training can be customized to address your specific needs and objectives.

What is the cost of your training programs?

Our pricing varies depending on the program, duration, and customization required. Public speaking workshops start from R1,500 per session, while corporate training is quoted based on specific requirements. Contact us for a detailed quote.

Do you offer online/virtual training options?

Absolutely! We offer both in-person and virtual training options to accommodate different preferences and locations. Our virtual sessions are interactive and designed to deliver the same impactful results as our in-person programs.

How do I book a consultation or assessment session?

You can book a consultation by filling out our contact form, calling our office, or emailing us directly. We'll schedule a convenient time to discuss your goals and recommend the best approach for your communication development.
