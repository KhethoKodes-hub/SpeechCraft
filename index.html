Who We Are

A social enterprise with purpose since 2023

Our Story

Founded in 2023, SpeechCraft Academy is a distinguished social enterprise dedicated to transforming individuals into confident, articulate, and impactful communicators. We deliver high-quality public speaking, leadership, facilitation, and entrepreneurial communication training.

Vision

To empower individuals to become a generation that is articulate, informed, and confident, capable of influencing positive change through powerful communication.

Mission

We aim to boost speakers' self-confidence, nurture critical thinking, and foster research and preparation skills that enable individuals to lead compelling conversations.

Our Services

Comprehensive programs designed for your success

Public Speaking & Leadership

  • Comprehensive courses covering speech writing, delivery techniques, and audience engagement
  • Programs tailored for student leaders at universities and FET colleges
  • Focused workshops for both beginners and seasoned speakers
  • Personalized one-on-one coaching with targeted feedback

Business Pitching & Proposals

  • Investor pitching workshops for entrepreneurs seeking funding
  • Proposal writing programs for grants and corporate collaborations
  • Start-up communication coaching for strategic presentations
  • Negotiation and persuasive communication skills

Facilitation Training

  • Effective facilitation mastery for youth development centers
  • Dynamic discussion-leading skills for educators and professionals
  • Managing diverse viewpoints and fostering collaboration
  • Interactive workshop methodologies
