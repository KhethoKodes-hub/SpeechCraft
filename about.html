Our Story

Founded in 2023, SpeechCraft Academy is a distinguished social enterprise dedicated to transforming individuals into confident, articulate, and impactful communicators. We deliver high-quality public speaking, leadership, facilitation, and entrepreneurial communication training.

Operating with a dual mission of professional excellence and social responsibility, we reinvest a significant portion of our profits into the SpeechCraft Academy Foundation (Non-Profit Company). This foundation supports public schools by establishing book reading clubs, debates, public speaking programs, and youth development initiatives.
