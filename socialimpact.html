The Speechcraft Academy Foundation

At the heart of our social impact mission is the Speechcraft Academy Foundation, a registered Non-Profit Company (NPC) dedicated to empowering youth in underserved communities through communication skills development.

Through the Foundation, we channel a significant portion of our profits into public school programs that focus on establishing and strengthening:

  • Book reading clubs to foster literacy and critical thinking
  • Debate societies to develop analytical and reasoning skills
  • Public speaking programs to build confidence and self-expression
  • Poetry and creative writing initiatives
  • Entrepreneurship and digital skills training for youth

Our model ensures that for every corporate training program or individual course purchased, we're able to extend our impact to learners who would otherwise not have access to these transformative skills.

Our Foundation Programs

We implement targeted programs designed to develop confident communicators and future leaders

Reading & Literacy Clubs

We establish and support book reading clubs in public schools to foster a love for reading, improve literacy rates, and develop critical thinking skills among learners.

Debate Societies

Our debate programs teach students to construct logical arguments, consider multiple perspectives, and articulate their thoughts clearly and persuasively.

Public Speaking Initiatives

We organize public speaking competitions and workshops that help students overcome stage fright and develop confidence in expressing their ideas.

Creative Expression

Through poetry slams, storytelling sessions, and creative writing workshops, we encourage students to find and use their unique voices.

Digital Skills Training

We provide basic digital literacy and online communication skills training to prepare youth for the modern workplace.

Youth Entrepreneurship

Our entrepreneurship programs teach business communication, pitching ideas, and developing persuasive presentation skills.
