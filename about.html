Our Story

Founded in 2023, SpeechCraft Academy is a distinguished social enterprise dedicated to transforming individuals into confident, articulate, and impactful communicators. We deliver high-quality public speaking, leadership, facilitation, and entrepreneurial communication training, while making a meaningful contribution to youth development and social progress.

Operating with a dual mission of professional excellence and social responsibility, we reinvest a significant portion of our profits into the SpeechCraft Academy Foundation (Non-Profit Company). This foundation supports public schools by establishing and strengthening book reading clubs, debates, public speaking programs, poetry initiatives, and other communication-based youth development projects.

We believe that powerful communication is the key to unlocking potential and driving positive change in communities.
