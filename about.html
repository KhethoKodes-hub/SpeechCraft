About Speechcraft Academy

Discover our story, mission, and the passionate team behind our mission to empower voices and inspire leaders across South Africa.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, Speechcraft Academy emerged from a simple but powerful observation: the ability to communicate effectively is one of the most transformative skills an individual can possess, yet it remains out of reach for many.

Our founder, Amanda Nkuna, witnessed firsthand how confident communication could unlock opportunities in both professional and personal contexts. Drawing from years of experience in education and corporate training, she established Speechcraft Academy with a dual mission: to provide world-class communication training while making a tangible social impact.

What began as a small initiative has grown into a respected social enterprise that has trained hundreds of individuals and partnered with numerous organizations, all while supporting youth development in underserved communities through our Foundation.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To boost speakers' self-confidence, nurture critical thinking, and foster research and preparation skills that enable individuals to lead compelling conversations and drive positive change in their communities.

Our Vision

To empower individuals to become a generation that is articulate, informed, and confident, capable of influencing positive change through powerful communication in all spheres of society.

Our Social Enterprise Model

Speechcraft Academy operates on a unique social enterprise model that combines professional excellence with meaningful social impact. We believe that business success and social responsibility are not mutually exclusive but rather complementary forces for positive change.

Through this innovative approach, we reinvest a significant portion of our profits into the Speechcraft Academy Foundation, a registered Non-Profit Company that supports public schools by establishing and strengthening communication-based youth development programs.
