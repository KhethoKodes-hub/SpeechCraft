Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you respond to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call us directly at 071 123 0001.

Do you offer customized training programs?

Yes, we specialize in creating tailored training programs that address your specific organizational needs and challenges. Our corporate training is completely customizable.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing varies based on the type of training, duration, and number of participants. We offer individual sessions, group workshops, and comprehensive corporate packages. Contact us for a detailed quote.

Can you conduct training at our location?

Absolutely! We offer both on-site and virtual training options to accommodate your preferences and logistical needs.

How does your social enterprise model work?

As a social enterprise, we reinvest a significant portion of our profits into the Speechcraft Academy Foundation, which supports youth development programs in public schools. When you train with us, you're also contributing to social impact.
