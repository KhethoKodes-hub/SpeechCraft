Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How soon can I start a training program?

Most of our programs have rolling admissions, and you can typically start within 1-2 weeks of registration. For corporate training programs, we usually schedule sessions 3-4 weeks in advance to allow for proper customization.

Do you offer virtual training options?

Yes, we offer both in-person and virtual training options. Our virtual sessions are interactive and designed to provide the same level of engagement and personal feedback as our in-person programs.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that schedules can change. For individual sessions, we require 24 hours notice for cancellations. For workshop registrations, you can cancel up to 7 days in advance for a full refund. Corporate training cancellations are handled on a case-by-case basis as outlined in our service agreements.

Do you offer customized programs for organizations?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating customized communication training programs tailored to your organization's specific needs, culture, and objectives. Contact us to schedule a consultation where we can discuss your requirements in detail.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and for corporate clients, we can arrange invoicing with payment terms. All pricing is transparent with no hidden fees.
